Use the following fields to directly download U.S. utility patents and published patent applications from Google Patents, and to directly download .zip files of their prosecution histories from the USPTO public data-proxy-repositors, Google & ReedTech. To automatically parse a prosecution history, drag the downloaded .zip file over the Patent PhD panel to the right on the main screen, and drop the file within the dropzone once it has become marked by a dotted border. The .zip file will then automatically upload to our servers, where LEO will generate a Patent PhD Report and return it to the main display screen for your review.
